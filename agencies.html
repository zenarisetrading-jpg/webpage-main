The agency problem

You're doing the work.
You just can't prove it.

Amazon PPC agencies are drowning in work that clients can't see. Every week your team is making dozens of bid changes, keyword adjustments, and campaign optimizations — and most of it is invisible to the client paying the retainer.

The result is a structural accountability problem: your value is real, but it's unverifiable. At renewal time, you're negotiating on narrative instead of evidence. Saddl was built to fix that with AI-powered counterfactual measurement.

Renewal conversations feel like negotiations

You know your work drove results. Proving it is another matter. Without a verified dollar figure, every renewal becomes a debate about whether the numbers were seasonal or your doing.

Reports show activity, not impact

ACOS went from 32% to 28%. Impressive — but how much money did that save? What was the incremental revenue? Clients who think in dollars don't relate to percentages as proof of value.

Team coordination breaks at scale

Managing 20+ client accounts across a team means decisions fall through the cracks. Who changed what, for which client, on which date — and did it work? Without a decision log, you're managing blind.
