Where Teikametrics Falls Short

Teikametrics is one of the more sophisticated Amazon PPC tools. The issue isn't capability — it's accountability.

Gap #1

AI Decisions Are Still Opaque

Teikametrics' AI adjusts bids based on its own models — but it doesn't expose the reasoning. When results improve, you don't know if it was the AI's bid change, organic rank movement, or a competitor going out of stock.

Gap #2

No Baseline Comparison

Teikametrics shows you that performance improved. It doesn't tell you what "baseline" performance would have been without any intervention. Without a counterfactual, you can't know if your decisions helped or if the market just moved.

Gap #3

Spend-Linked Pricing Misaligns Incentives

When your tool charges a percentage of ad spend, it has no financial incentive to help you reduce waste. Saddl's flat pricing means we only win when you do — by finding inefficiencies, not growing them.

What Saddl AdPulse Does Differently

Every decision. Every outcome. Traced, measured, verified.

Advantage #1

Outcome-to-Decision Traceability

Each optimization you make — bid adjustment, negative keyword, match type change — is logged and measured individually. When revenue moves, you see exactly which decision caused it and by how much.

Advantage #2

Market Baseline Separation

We calculate what your account's performance would have been without your changes, based on market trends. Then we measure your actual performance against that baseline. That gap — positive or negative — is your true impact.

Advantage #3

Aligned Incentives, Flat Pricing

We charge a flat rate, not a percentage of spend. That means we're incentivized to help you find wasted spend and double down on what works — not to keep your ad budget as high as possible.

The Explainability Gap in
AI-First Optimization

Teikametrics relies on powerful AI models to automate bidding and keyword discovery. While their algorithms are sophisticated, they often suffer from the **explainability gap**. When an AI makes 500 bid changes across your account in a single day, it can't tell you the individual "why" behind each one, let alone provide a verified revenue impact for each specific adjustment.

AdPulse takes a "White Box" approach. We believe that for an optimization to be valuable, it must be accountable. Instead of a black-box AI pushing buttons, AdPulse provides a transparent decision log where every change is timestamped, justified with a forecast delta, and measured against a counterfactual baseline. You don't have to trust "the machine"—you can see the math.

Moreover, Teikametrics' focus on Flywheel dynamics is useful for the initial growth phase, but as a brand matures, ROI becomes the only metric that matters. AdPulse is built specifically for this transition, helping mature brands strip away the "AI noise" to find the pure, incremental yield in their accounts.

Why transparency beats AI automation:

  • ✓
    Audit-Ready Decisions: Every bid change in AdPulse includes a confidence score and a rationale, so your team knows exactly why a change was made and can defend it to stakeholders.
  • ✓
    Verification over Guesswork: We track the revenue 'delta' for 60 days following every change, proving exactly how much money a decision made (or lost) in real-world market conditions.
  • ✓
    Strategic Learning: By identifying which specific optimizations actually worked, we help you avoid repeating costly bid strategies that the market doesn't reward.
