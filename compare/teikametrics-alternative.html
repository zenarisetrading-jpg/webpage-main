Where Teikametrics Falls Short

Teikametrics is one of the more sophisticated Amazon PPC tools. The issue isn't capability — it's accountability.

Gap #1

AI Decisions Are Still Opaque

Teikametrics' AI adjusts bids based on its own models — but it doesn't expose the reasoning. When results improve, you don't know if it was the AI's bid change, organic rank movement, or a competitor going out of stock.

Gap #2

No Baseline Comparison

Teikametrics shows you that performance improved. It doesn't tell you what "baseline" performance would have been without any intervention. Without a counterfactual, you can't know if your decisions helped or if the market just moved.

Gap #3

Spend-Linked Pricing Misaligns Incentives

When your tool charges a percentage of ad spend, it has no financial incentive to help you reduce waste. Saddl's flat pricing means we only win when you do — by finding inefficiencies, not growing them.

What Saddl AdPulse Does Differently

Every decision. Every outcome. Traced, measured, verified.

Advantage #1

Outcome-to-Decision Traceability

Each optimization you make — bid adjustment, negative keyword, match type change — is logged and measured individually. When revenue moves, you see exactly which decision caused it and by how much.

Advantage #2

Market Baseline Separation

We calculate what your account's performance would have been without your changes, based on market trends. Then we measure your actual performance against that baseline. That gap — positive or negative — is your true impact.

Advantage #3

Aligned Incentives, Flat Pricing

We charge a flat rate, not a percentage of spend. That means we're incentivized to help you find wasted spend and double down on what works — not to keep your ad budget as high as possible.
