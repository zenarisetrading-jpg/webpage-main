Where Perpetua Falls Short

To be fair, Perpetua is a solid bidding automation tool. But automation alone doesn't answer the most important question.

Gap #1

Automation ≠ Accountability

Perpetua's AI adjusts bids automatically — but when sales move, you can't tell if it was the bid change, a market trend, or something else entirely. You get results without reasons.

Gap #2

No Incrementality Measurement

If your sales grew 12% during a period when the whole category grew 15%, your "optimization" actually underperformed. Perpetua won't tell you that. It shows growth, not incremental growth.

Gap #3

Hard to Justify ROI Internally

When your CFO asks "what did we get for our PPC spend this month?", Perpetua can't give a clean answer tied to specific decisions. You're left piecing together a story from dashboards.
