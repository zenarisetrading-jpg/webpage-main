Where Helium 10 Adtomic Falls Short

Helium 10 is excellent for keyword research, listing optimization, and competitor analysis. PPC attribution is a different problem.

Gap #1

A Feature, Not a Focus

Adtomic is one component of a 30+ feature platform built around keyword research and listing tools. PPC attribution requires deep focus. When it's a feature, it gets feature-level depth — not product-level depth.

Gap #2

Standard Metrics, No Incrementality

Adtomic shows ACoS, ROAS, clicks, and conversions — the same metrics you get from Amazon itself. What it doesn't show is whether your specific bid or keyword changes actually caused the results, or if the market moved anyway.

Gap #3

You Pay for the Whole Suite

If all you need is decision-level PPC attribution, you're paying for Helium 10's entire feature set to access Adtomic. Saddl AdPulse is standalone — you only pay for the PPC measurement capability you actually need.
