How it works

Connected, automated,
and fully accountable.

01

Connect your Amazon account

Link AdPulse directly to your Amazon Ads and Selling Partner accounts in under 2 minutes. No CSV exports, no manual downloads — your campaigns and sales data sync automatically. Prefer manual? You can still upload your Search Term Report instead.

Amazon Ads API SP-API ~2 minutes
02

Review what needs to change

AdPulse analyses your account data and surfaces three types of actions: search terms wasting spend that need blocking, high-performing terms buried in Auto campaigns ready for harvest, and keyword bids that are misaligned with your ROAS targets.

Waste detection Keyword harvest Bid intelligence
03

Changes go live — automatically

Keyword bid optimizations are pushed directly to Amazon the moment they're confirmed. No bulk file downloads, no Seller Central uploads. Budget changes and new campaign launches require your approval before going live.

Bids — automatic Budget & campaigns — approval required
04

See exactly what made money

At 14, 30, and 60 days, AdPulse measures the actual dollar impact of each decision against a counterfactual baseline — what would have happened without that change. The result is a decision log that proves your value in dollars, not direction.

14/30/60-day attribution Counterfactual baseline
