The Method

Counterfactual intelligence,
not credit-claiming.

We don't just measure what happened. We model what would have happened without each decision — and report the difference.

01

Log every decision

Every bid change, keyword addition, negative, and campaign structure update is captured with a timestamp, context, and expected outcome.

02

Build the counterfactual

Our AI-powered model constructs what the revenue curve would have looked like without that decision — using your historical data and market signals.

03

Verify and report

The gap between actual and counterfactual is your proven impact. Verified, timestamped, and ready for a client report in one click.
