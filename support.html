Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is counterfactual measurement?

Counterfactual measurement is the gold standard of causal inference. Instead of just looking at what happened (ROAS), we model what *would have happened* if you hadn't made a specific change. The difference between the actual revenue and the counterfactual baseline is your true incremental impact.

Is Saddl AdPulse an automation tool?

We are a measurement and decision intelligence platform. While we provide bid adjustment recommendations and harvest winners automatically, our core value is the Accountability Loop—showing you exactly which of those automations (and your manual changes) actually made money.

How long does it take to see the first results?

You'll see historical analysis within 10 minutes of connecting your Amazon account. For live decision-tracking, AdPulse needs about 72 hours of data to establish a high-confidence baseline for new optimizations.

Do you support Amazon UAE and Saudi Arabia?

Yes. As a company based in Dubai, we have full support for Amazon AE and SA, alongside major global marketplaces like USA, UK, and Europe. Our models are specifically tuned for both high-velocity and emerging marketplace dynamics.

How does the agency white-label reporting work?

Agencies can generate PDF reports with their own branding that focus on "Proven Impact." Instead of confusing clients with ROAS fluctuations, you show them the verified dollar amount your agency added to their bottom line this month.

Billing & Policies

Subscription & Cancellation

How does billing work?

Saddl AdPulse is billed monthly with a minimum three-month commitment to ensure enough time for our AI to establish baselines and prove ROI. For agency partners, client fees are consolidated into a single monthly invoice.

Can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time after your initial three-month minimum term directly through the dashboard. Once the commitment period is met, your account switches to a standard month-to-month plan.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 14-day free trial on all plans to ensure AdPulse is the right fit for your business. Once a billing cycle has been processed, we do not offer prorated refunds, but you will retain full access to the platform for the remainder of the month.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. For established agency partners with high client volume, we also offer bank transfer (ACH/Wire) options and custom billing cycles.
