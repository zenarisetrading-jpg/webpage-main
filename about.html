Who We Are

About Us

SADDL AdPulse is an Amazon PPC analytics and optimization platform developed and operated by Zenarise Trading LLC FZ, a technology and ecommerce company incorporated in the United Arab Emirates (Business Registration No. 242017801).

The platform provides Amazon sellers and agencies with campaign performance analytics, counterfactual impact measurement, and demand-adjusted bid optimization across Amazon marketplaces.

SADDL AdPulse was built by active Amazon and Noon marketplace sellers. Zenarise Trading LLC FZ operates its own seller accounts across UAE and Saudi Arabia marketplaces, giving the platform's optimization methodology a foundation in real advertiser experience.

    The Foundation

    The Problem That Started Everything.

    I've been selling on Amazon for years. Built a 7-figure business. Spent hundreds of thousands on PPC.

    And for most of that time, I had no idea if my optimizations were actually working.

    Every week, I'd make bid changes. Block search terms. Launch new campaigns. Harvest keywords. I was doing the work. But when someone asked "is all that PPC effort paying off?"—I couldn't really answer.

    Which of my optimizations actually worked?

    I could look at overall ROAS, sure. But that doesn't tell you which decisions drove results. Did that bid increase help? Did those negative keywords save money? Or was it just market timing?

    So I built the tool I wished existed. That's AdPulse.

    What We Believe

    Feedback loops change everything

    Without measurement, there's no learning. Without learning, there's no compounding. Most sellers make the same mistakes over and over because they never find out which decisions hurt them. AdPulse exists to close that loop.

    Activity isn't the same as results

    The industry has convinced everyone that more optimizations = better performance. But volume of activity isn't quality of decisions. The best PPC managers aren't the ones who make the most changes—they're the ones who know which changes to make.

    You deserve to see what worked

    Black box tools ask you to trust their algorithms. We think you should see exactly which decisions drove revenue, which ones didn't, and why. Transparency isn't a feature—it's a requirement.

    Proof beats promises

    We don't ask you to trust us. We show you the data. Every optimization gets tracked. Every outcome gets measured. You see exactly what happened.
